The Best Movies According to Ryan

My top 5 movies of all-time. (in no particular order)

1. The Conjuring 2

I love the whole series but i found this to be the scariest

2. Wedding Crashers

So funny, I could and have watched it over and over.

3. Love Me if You Dare

A french romantic dark comedy. It's just so well done, it tugged on all my emotions

4. Midsommer

An absolute masterpiece. Visually stunning, insanely beautiful plot. This one deeply affected my soul

5. Disney's Robin Hood

I can remember watching this all the time as a young one.
